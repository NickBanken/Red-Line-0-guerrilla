About us

Urbex Engineering Limited has been in operation since 1978

as a General Contractor, Project Manager, Consulting Engineer, and Manager of Land Development. Since 1985 Urbex Engineering Limited has been actively involved in providing municipal design services for a wide range of clients.

Urbex Engineering Limited is presently active in municipal engineering services for approximately twenty-five hundred residential lots a number of multi-residential, commercial and institutional site plans as well as the management of approximately 300 acres of development property.

General-Contractor

When it comes to general contracting,

it can be a little bit confusing for those not in the industry to understand the complexity of what they do.
